A brief History

The Monster hunter was introduce in 2004 only for the ps2 (playStation 2). However, even though the the franchise itself has been out since 2004, many people still haven't respect its franchise until MHW (monster hunter world). MHW sold over 17 billions copies worldwide making it capcoms biggest game sold and finally making the monster hunter franchise finally known in the gaming community.
